Max & Matilda's Idiot Sandwich Guide to Warsaw 🥪💋✨

🥪 About Us

We met last week. Now we’re in Warsaw. No plan, just instinct. Good food, better company, and a soft commitment to indulging in absolutely everything — dumplings, jazz, each other. This guide is what happens when two dangerously compatible people put their weekend in the hands of hunger, curiosity, and perfectly timed touches. We took notes. You’re welcome. ✨🍸📸

✈️ SATURDAY — Pierogi & Power Plays 🥟💃

10:00 AM – Arrival: 🧳 Touchdown in Warsaw. The energy is flirty, electric, and very much giving “we’re not from here, but we *should* be.”

11:00 AM – Drop bags & freshen up: 💄 A quick change. A shared glance. You both look good — too good for check-in.

12:30 PM – LUNCH: 🍽️ Polish Comfort Food, Elevated
🥟 Main: Zapiecek – duck & cranberry pierogi, zurek soup.
🥔 Local Dive: Pyzy Flaki Gorące – potato dumpling chaos.
🍸 Wild Card: Specjały Regionalne – rustic, vodka between courses, Matilda wins the order battle.

2:00 PM – Old Town Stroll: 🏰 Cobblestones. Chimney cakes. Fingers brushing. The kind of quiet that says a lot.

4:00 PM – Cake & Chill:
🍰 Café Bristol – all chandeliers & whispers.
🌿 Same Fusy – herbal, witchy, charmingly weird. Cheesecake required.

5:30 PM – Afternoon Break: 🛏️ 90 minutes of slow, glowy decompression. No interruptions. Just you, the sheets, and one well-timed "don't stop." 🔥

7:30 PM – DINNER: 🍷 Polish Glamour
🍷 Stary Dom – mushroom soup, eye contact, candlelight.
🖤 Rozbrat 20 – sleek, elegant, you linger between courses and under the table.

10:00 PM – Cocktails at Weles: 🍸 Hidden. Velvet. Bison grass vodka. You sit close and talk low.

12:00 AM – Sexy Wind Down: 🕯️ Soft light. Slow hands. One of you starts laughing mid-kiss. The other doesn’t mind. 🫦

☀️ SUNDAY — Deep Cuts & Dirty Thoughts 🧠🔥

9:30 AM – Slow Morning: 🍩 A. Blikle donuts in bed. Someone puts on music. Someone else forgets to get up. 🛌

11:30 AM – POLIN Museum: 🏛️ Architectural. Moving. You wander together, then alone, then together again. Quiet in a good way.

1:30 PM – LUNCH: 🍲 Real Polish Realness
🥬 Bar Prasowy – zero frills, full flavor.
📦 Bar Gdański – even more local. Even more unforgettable.

3:00 PM – Łazienki Park: 🌳 Swans. Peacocks. Music benches playing Chopin. You talk nonsense like it’s poetry.

5:00 PM – Hotel Interlude: 🔥 You lose track of time. And limbs. No one cares.

7:30 PM – DINNER: 🥃 Vodka Wonderland
🥃 Dom Wódki – five vodkas, three courses, two people increasingly close.
🫧 NUTA – smoke, foam, and edible questions. You love it.

10:00 PM – Jazz or Rooftop?
🎷 Jassmine – soft sax, even softer lighting.
🌃 Level 27 – high above Warsaw. Max finally dances. Matilda claps once.

1:00 AM – Return: 💤 You say little. You mean everything.

⛅ MONDAY — Cool & Connected 🎧🧳

9:30 AM – Charlotte Breakfast: 🥐 Prosecco. Jam. The best croissant you’ve ever had. Matilda wears sunglasses inside again. Max doesn’t even flinch.

11:00 AM – Neon Museum + Praga: 💡 Neon signs, art, and a final coffee from somewhere that feels like a secret. You barely speak. In a good way.

2:00 PM – LUNCH: 🍷 Stylish Last Bite
🍷 Ale Wino – tucked-away perfection. A bottle split. A toast to doing this again. Maybe. No pressure.

4:00 PM – Final Walk: 🧦 Matching socks? Probably. Matching energy? Definitely.

6:00 PM – Airport: ✈️ Still full. In all the best ways.
