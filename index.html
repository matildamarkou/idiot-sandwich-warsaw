Max & Matilda's Idiot Sandwich Guide to Warsaw 🥪💋

A slightly unhinged, culturally refined, dumpling-fueled, one-week-old situationship survival guide.

✈️ SATURDAY — “Pierogi & Power Plays”

10:00 AM – Arrival
Touchdown in Warsaw. You’re giving: international mystery meets cheeky jetlag.

11:00 AM – Drop bags, freshen up
Fresh fits. Fresh energy. Maybe a pregame prosecco.

12:30 PM – LUNCH: Polish Comfort Food, Elevated
Main Pick: Zapiecek 🥟 – Classic Polish dumplings. It's cozy chaos.
Get: Duck & cranberry pierogi, zurek in a bread bowl.
Local Alt: Pyzy Flaki Gorące – no frills, no tourists, pure potato-dumpling joy.
Cool Girl Pick: Specjały Regionalne – rustic but refined, with shots of ice-cold vodka between bites.

2:00 PM – Old Town Stroll
Cobblestones, castle square, those romantic amber shops that look haunted. Grab a chimney cake. Eat it messily. Laugh like you’ve never been heartbroken.

4:00 PM – Cake & Chill 🍰
Café Bristol: bougie, beautiful, big “this is where the diplomats flirt” energy
Same Fusy: witchy, charming, and excellent for people-watching
Order: Polish cheesecake or szarlotka (warm apple pie), espresso, and a gossip session

5:30 PM – Afternoon Decompression (and De-robing) 🛏️
You’ve been classy. Now be messy. Block 90 mins for horizontal recreation. A lil silly, a lil steamy, all consensual.

7:30 PM – DINNER: Polish Glamour
Main Pick: Stary Dom – elegant, candlelit, the kind of place you order wild game and pretend you own a countryside estate.
Alt Option: Rozbrat 20 – modern fine dining with Polish soul. Smooth, smart, sexy.

10:00 PM – Cocktails at Weles 🍸
Hidden entrance. Velvet. Candles. Feels like something’s about to happen — and it probably will. Order a drink with bison grass vodka. Pretend you’re spies on your night off.

12:00 AM – Sexy Wind Down 🔥
Shoes off. Shirts off. Music on. Optional: dramatic lighting. Required: enthusiastic consent. You both sleep like royalty. Or at least really hot peasants.

☀️ SUNDAY — “Deep Cuts & Dirty Thoughts”

9:30 AM – Breakfast in bed (or from bakery)
A. Blikle donuts or croissants from Charlotte. Eat half-dressed while debating something like “Would you survive in a commune?” Optional: morning sex. Not required, but heavily encouraged.

11:30 AM – Culture Hit: POLIN Museum 🏛️
Thoughtful, moving, one of the best in Europe. You’ll whisper things like “damn, this is intense” and “we’re so cultured.”

1:30 PM – LUNCH: Real Polish Realness
Main Pick: Bar Prasowy – authentic milk bar. No pretense, all flavor.
Get: gołąbki (stuffed cabbage), bigos, kompot
Deep Cut Alt: Bar Gdański – more off-grid, more Warsaw. Super local.

3:00 PM – Łazienki Park Walk 🌳
You + swans + Chopin benches playing piano music = serene but sexy. Sit. Flirt. Debate who would win in a knife fight. Light PDA optional.

5:00 PM – Recharge & Round 3 💦
You’ve earned it. Take it slow. Try new angles. Maybe throw in a playlist that starts ironic and ends spiritual.

7:30 PM – DINNER: Vodka Wonderland 🥃
Main Pick: Dom Wódki – every course has a vodka pairing. You’ll leave tipsy, happy, and dangerously flirty.
Wild Card: NUTA – molecular Polish-Asian fusion. Foam. Smoke. Mystery. Delicious nonsense.

10:00 PM – Late Night Fun 🌃
Jassmine: underground jazz bar. Slinky, smoky, sexy.
Level 27: rooftop club. Great cocktails, better views. Potential for wild dancing or people-watching.

1:00 AM – Sexy, Satisfied Sleep 😴
You whisper something deep. They pretend not to hear it. Both of you feel electric.

⛅ MONDAY — “Cool & Connected”

9:30 AM – Breakfast at Charlotte 🥐
Prosecco, jam, and people-watching. You both look slightly disheveled in a hot way. Passersby are jealous.

11:00 AM – Neon Museum + Praga Walk 🧙
Neon signs + old warehouses = perfect morning zone-out. Grab coffee from Stor or Café Sztuka i Kawa.

2:00 PM – LUNCH: Stylish Last Bite 🍷
Pick: Ale Wino – hidden courtyard, modern Polish food, excellent wine. It’s your “this weekend was hot and weird and maybe genius” meal.

4:00 PM – Final Stroll on Nowy Świat 🛍️
Souvenirs. Stolen kisses. Existential jokes. Maybe matching socks? Maybe just a chocolate bar you’ll pretend to forget to share.

6:00 PM – Airport ✈️
You leave full. In every way. Not in love. But goddamn, that was one for the books.
