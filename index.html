🥪 About Us

We met last week. We're in Warsaw this week. Are we in love? Absolutely not. Are we incredibly well-fed and constantly half-naked? Yes. This site is our tribute to 48 hours of dumplings, vodka, culture, and perfectly timed sex breaks. Welcome to our idiot sandwich guide to Poland’s capital.
